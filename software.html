Animation 1 you will be introduced to the process of animation. Whether you have worked in an animation software program or this is your first exposure, every student will be challenged and learn something new.

Adobe Animate CC 2020 is one of the most powerful tools you will be using to create your 2-D animations. Like other Adobe products you will find that Adobe Animates platform is similar to their other product.

During Distance Learning students that are taking Animation will be working in a program called Wick Editor. Along with this program, students will have access to the online version of Adobe Photoshop.
